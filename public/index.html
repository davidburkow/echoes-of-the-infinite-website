The Infinite

Some music is written. Some is remembered. Echoes of the Infinite lives in the space between hypnotic rhythms and emotional depth, where darker, late-night energy meets something more timeless. The music here echoes through dimensions while leaving behind a distinct memory. As the imprint label of BURKO, Echoes of the Infinite reflects a world shaped by the sonic unknown.

Echoes of the Infinite is the sonic representation of the feeling of "Deja vu." Rooted in BURKO's process of capturing song ideas from his dreams and translating them into reality, the label explores the deeper, darker, more emotional edges of electronic music. Tracks here are designed to leave a lasting imprint, but the defining factor is a feeling, not a genre.

At its core, the label is about curation. Its intention is to build a cohesive world where soundscapes, emotional depth, and visual identity all intersect. Echoes of the Infinite reflects BURKO's evolving sonic identity while opening the door to like-minded artists drawn to the same darker, esoteric sonic space.

BURKO

Labyrinth EP

BURKO's 'Labyrinth EP' sets the tone for Echoes of the Infinite as the label's first release, leaning into the darker tones he has brought through his music over the past 6 years of releases. With music previously on ARMADA, Ultra, mau5trap, and Odd One Out, this EP solidifies his new record label as a home for his darker-leaning records.

The title track reveals a high-energy, psy-leaning rhythm with a heavy bass line, while 'Delusion' and 'Planet Nine' manage to not let up on pumping energy. Perfect for your 3am journey, the EP takes you further down the 'Nightrider' playlist rabbit hole that has already been home to multiple of BURKO's previous releases.

The Artist

BURKO is building a future defined by melodic psy tech, marked by emotional depth and captivating energy. As his discography expands, his unique production style and collaborations continue to earn support from icons like Deadmau5, David Guetta, Vintage Culture, Lane 8, and YOTTO setting the stage for his rise in the global electronic scene.

His record label, Echoes of the Infinite, is the sonic representation of the feeling of "Déjà vu." Rooted in BURKO's process of capturing song ideas from his dreams and translating them into reality, the label explores the deeper, darker, more emotional edges of electronic music. Tracks here are designed to leave a lasting imprint, but the defining factor is a feeling, not a genre.

At its core, the label is about curation. Its intention is to build a cohesive world where soundscapes, emotional depth, and visual identity all intersect. Echoes of the Infinite reflects BURKO's evolving sonic identity while opening the door to like-minded artists drawn to the same darker, esoteric sonic space.
